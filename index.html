Voice Recognition

Voice Recognition is the identification of a person from characteristics of voices (voice biometrics). We can provide a service based authentication system that can seamleassly integrate into a lot of pre-existing IVR systems in use by companies already.


Quick Authentication

Since each person's voice can be reliably distinguished, this is a very effective system for identifying people for authentication purposes.

Financial Applications

This technology is getting increasingly popular with companies that need to verify identities over the phone, at the forefront of which are service providers in the financial sector, i.e. banks etc.
